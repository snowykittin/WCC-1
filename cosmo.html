Cosmetology

Did you know that you could be a hairstylist, make-up artist, nail tech, chemical specialist, and much more with only one license? With the successful completion of an application and interview process, excellent school attendance (95%), 2.0 GPA (C or higher), up to date with credits for graduation, you can be on your way to a promising career in Cosmetology. After a two year course at Walker Career Center, you will have completed 1,500 hours of combined theory and practical skills needed to attain your Indiana State cosmetology license. The cost for the necessary materials is $500.00 for the first year and $200.00 for the second year. Dual credit is awarded by Vincennes University for those meeting university requirements and successfully completing the course.
